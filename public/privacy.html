Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 5, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is handled when you use the IG Token Tool.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect or store any personal information on our servers. The tool acts as a pass-through to help you obtain an Instagram Access Token directly from Meta.

  • Instagram Data: When you sign in, Meta provides an access token. This token is used solely to display it to you in your browser.
  • Local Storage: We do not store tokens in cookies or local storage.

2. How We Use Information

Any information retrieved from Meta (like your access token) is only used to display it to you so you can copy and use it for your own development purposes.

3. Data Security

The access token is transmitted securely from Meta to your browser. We recommend that you never share your access token with unauthorized parties.

4. Third-Party Services

This tool uses the Meta Graph API. Your use of this tool is also subject to Meta's Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

5. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact the developer of this self-hosted tool.
